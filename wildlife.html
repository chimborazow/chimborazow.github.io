BEAUTIFUL PLACE

One of the world's most stunning volcanic geography and landscapes, with a huge diversity of flora and fauna. the islands have attracted a wide array of visitors over the centuries from pirates and seafarers to refugees and even Incas! Most famous of visitors to the Galapagos Islands is Charles Darwin whose observations of the island's birdlife led to his development of the Theory of Evolution. When you visit we can take on tour and take the "Charles Darwin Tour" which goes everywhere he went.
